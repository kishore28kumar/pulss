# Admin FAQs — Pulss White-Label Platform

## Product Management
**Q:** How do I bulk upload products?
**A:** Go to the Products tab in your admin dashboard, click “Bulk Upload,” download the CSV template, fill in your products and variants, and upload the file.

**Q:** Can I add product variants like strength, pack size, or brand?
**A:** Yes! Fill out the variant columns in your CSV (e.g., Strength, Pack Size) and group variants under the same Product ID.

**Q:** How do I upload images for products?
**A:** Use the drag-and-drop bulk image upload feature in the Products tab. You can upload multiple images per product or variant.

## Orders & Customers
**Q:** How do I get notified for new orders?
**A:** New orders trigger an in-app ring/alert in your dashboard. You’ll see a timer and an “Accept” button. If not accepted in time, orders are auto-accepted.

**Q:** Can I contact customers via WhatsApp?
**A:** Yes! If you’ve provided your WhatsApp number during onboarding, customers can click the WhatsApp chat button on your store and product pages.

## Payments
**Q:** How do I set up UPI and Razorpay payments?
**A:** Enter your UPI ID and Razorpay merchant details during onboarding or in your store profile settings. These options will be shown to customers during checkout.

**Q:** Can I accept Cash on Delivery or Credit payments?
**A:** Yes, enable these options in your store profile. You can manage credit ledger and limits from the dashboard.

## Branding & Theming
**Q:** How can I change my store’s theme, logo, or branding?
**A:** Use the Branding tab in your dashboard to upload logos, select themes, and adjust colors and fonts.

## Support & Feedback
**Q:** Where can I get technical help or report issues?
**A:** Use the Feedback widget in your dashboard for suggestions, bugs, or support. You can also contact your super admin.

## Accessibility & Internationalization
**Q:** Can I add more languages or improve accessibility?
**A:** Yes, contact your super admin to add new language files or request accessibility enhancements.
